描述

This class is for children ages 9-16 years old. Your child will learn the basics of the French language, with a focus on spoken communication. The textbook, Adomania, is aimed at young learners of the 21st century. It provides a fun glimpse into the life of middle-school students in Clermont-Ferrand, France. I lived, worked, and studied for a year in this city, so I have much insight to offer to your child. The book is available in China.
